How to Dress for Different Vacation Destinations

Each travel destination has its own fashion vibe. Tropical locations like Bali and Maldives call for light, breathable fabrics such as linen and cotton.

For city vacations like Paris or Dubai, opt for chic, structured outfits with neutral tones and elegant silhouettes.

The key is to balance comfort and style while matching the atmosphere of your destination.
